Floats

What is a Float?

An ice cream float is any drink poured over two scoops of ice cream. As a result of the drink being poured over the ice cream, a rich, frothy foam is created that is heavenly to sip from the top of your cup.

Adding ice cream to a drink makes the drink rich and creamy. If you have never tried a float, stop by and give one a try. You will not be disappointed!

Our Ice Cream

We use high-quality ingredients to make our ice cream. We combine fresh cream and natural flavorings to create a heavenly scoop of ice cream.

We make twenty flavors of ice cream daily. Our ice cream is made with the ingredients that we have around the shop on any given day. Since we have an enormous amount of ingredients around the shop, you never know what ice cream creations the staff will come up with each day. We will always have vanilla, chocolate, strawberry, and coffee flavored ice creams available.

Floats We Serve

We make our floats using any of our soft drink, coffee, tea, or juice flavors. Choose your favorite drink, and we will pour it over two large scoops of ice cream. If you would like whipped cream to top off your delicious drink, we can do that.
