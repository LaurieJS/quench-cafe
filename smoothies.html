Smoothies

Our Produce

Our fruits and vegetables are purchased from local organic farms. We verify that the farms are pesticide-free and do not use GMO seeds. We also purchase our non-seasonal fruits and vegetables from trusted farms around the world.

Our customers can take comfort in the fact that we strictly purchase produce from pesticide-free and non-GMO farms.

How are the smoothies made?

We make our smoothies with the freshest fruits and vegetables. We have a fruit and vegetable bar where customers can pick out their own fruits and vegetables. Once the customer brings the produce to the counter, we will blend the fruits and vegetables with yogurt, freshly squeezed juice and juice ice cubes to create their refreshing masterpiece.

If customers do not want to pick out their own fruits and vegetables, they can simply tell us which produce they would like, and our staff will blend it to perfection.

Smoothies We Serve

All of our smoothies are made to order to your exact specifications, which we will be more than happy to top with whipped cream. If you have not tried a watermelon smoothie, stop on by and give one a try, they are so refreshing! We also offer dairy alternatives for vegans.
