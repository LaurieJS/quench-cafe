Shakes

About Our Shakes

The Quench Cafe makes a wide variety of shakes. We use our freshly made ice cream, milk, and a flavoring of your choice, which we blend into a creamy, delicious drink.

We also offer candy bars, cookies, brownies, and pound cake that can be blended into your drinkable masterpiece.

Our Ice Cream

We use high-quality ingredients to make our ice cream. We combine fresh cream and natural flavorings to create a heavenly scoop of ice cream.

We make twenty flavors of ice cream daily. Our ice cream is made with the ingredients that we have around the shop on any given day. Since we have an enormous amount of ingredients around the shop, you never know what ice cream creations the staff will come up with each day. We will always have vanilla, chocolate, strawberry, and coffee flavored ice creams available.

Shakes We Serve

We make our shakes according to your specifications. Try the Strawberry Shortcake shake, which is filled with fresh strawberries, chunks of pound cake, and our strawberry ice cream topped with a generous amount of whipped cream, drizzled with strawberry sauce. You will not be disappointed!
