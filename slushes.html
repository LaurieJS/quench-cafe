Slushes

About Our Slushes

Who does not enjoy a large slush on a hot day? We make twenty slush flavors daily.

We also offer five sugar-free slush flavors daily, which are great for our customers with diabetes or who watching their intake of carbohydrates.

How do we make our slushes?

Each of the Quench Cafe locations has a wall of slush machines that make our delicious icy drinks throughout the day.

Whenever possible, we add our own fresh juices to the machines to create slushes that contain healthy vitamins and minerals.

Slushes We Serve

Our wide variety of slush flavors can be mixed together, or you can enjoy a large cup of your favorite flavor. You may also have your slush topped with fresh fruit or a scoop of ice cream, for a small additional charge.
