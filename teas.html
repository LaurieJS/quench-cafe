Teas

Where do we get our tea?

The Quench Cafe purchases our bulk whole-leaf teas from sustainable farms around the globe. We are committed to offering teas that are grown on farms that practice sustainability and are socially conscious.

We work hard to ensure that our teas are of the highest quality.

How do we prepare our tea?

We brew a variety of teas throughout the day to make our refreshing iced teas. Each day, we offer special blends of ice tea that our customers can choose from. Do not forget to visit the condiment bar, where you can flavor your tea to perfection.

To make our hot teas, the customer chooses the whole leaf teas that they would like, and we place the mixture into two specially made tea bags. Customers may steep their tea bags in a cup of hot water for as long, or little, as they wish. When your cup is empty, we will be more than happy to refill your cup with hot water.

Teas We Serve

We offer over twenty varieties of whole leaf teas, which our customers can choose from or combine. We can also add a scoop of ice cream and top it with whipped cream if you are in the mood to try something a little different, but delicious.
