Soft Drinks

Thirsty?

If you are thirsty, you have come to the right place. The Quench Cafe serves a wide variety of soft drinks that will definitely quench your thirst.

Customers may also request ice cubes made from our juices, teas, and coffees to add an extra punch to their soft drinks. Coke with coffee cubes…try it, it is amazing!

We Offer Coca-Cola Products

We are proud to use the Coca-Cola Freestyle machine, which dispenses over a hundred soft drink flavors. Whether you are in the mood for a vanilla Barq’s Root Beer or diet grape lemonade, we have got you covered.

We also have Coca-Cola Freestyle Machines in our dining areas where our customers can experiment and find their favorite mixture of soft drinks. Did we forget to mention that soft drink refills are free? While enjoying a drink in one of our spacious indoor dining areas, customers may refill their soft drinks at any time.

Soft Drinks We Serve

We serve every Coke product imaginable. Since the Coca-Cola Freestyle Machine offers such a wide variety of drinks, many customers like to grab their cups and head over to the machine to create drinks on their own. Kids love to create their own drinks using the Freestyle Machine!
