About Us

What is the Quench Cafe?

The Quench Cafe serves a wide variety of drinks from three locations in the San Francisco Bay area. Our goal is to make the highest quality drinks without breaking our customers' pocketbook.

We roast our own coffee beans, make our own ice cream, and use only the freshest ingredients, which cuts down on costs and in turn we can pass those savings on to you.

Our Drinks

All of our drinks are made with finest and freshest ingredients. We are proud to use non-GMO products because we are concerned about the health of the environment, as well as our customers.

In addition, we use sustainable products whenever possible, and attempt to keep our carbon footprint to a minimum. The Earth and its inhabitants are very important to us.

Our Cafes

Each of our Cafes offers a comfortable dine-in seating area with free Wi-Fi and a relaxing atmosphere to sit and enjoy your drinks with friends and family. Our Cafes also offer drive-thru service for the times when you are in a hurry, and in need of a drink.
