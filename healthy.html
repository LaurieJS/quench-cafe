Healthy Drinks

Where do get our produce?

Our fruits and vegetables are purchased from local organic farms. We verify that the farms are pesticide-free and do not use GMO seeds. We also purchase our non-seasonal fruits and vegetables from trusted farms around the world.

Our customers can take comfort in the fact that we strictly purchase produce from pesticide-free and non-GMO farms.

How do we make our juice?

We freshly squeeze our fruit and vegetable juices throughout the day. Customers that enter the shop will more than likely witness our staff members in the process of squeezing juice. We use large electronic juicers to make our delicious juices, which removes all traces of pulp. Leaving behind a fruity liquid gold that is full of vitamins and minerals.

To ensure freshness, if we do not use our juices within an hour of being squeezed, then they are made into juice ice cubes. That way we can pour our freshly squeezed juices over ice and our customers will not need to worry about their drinks becoming watered down.

Healthy Drinks We Serve

We will juice any produce that we can fit into our juicers. If you would like to pick out your own combination of fruits and vegetables, we will happily make a drink to your specifications. We also serve delicious protein drinks.
